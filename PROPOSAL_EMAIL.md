## Notes for you (not part of the email)

- **Anchor on the full value first.** The $8,500 is the real, defensible price —
  keep it visible so the discount reads as the favor it is, not your baseline.
- **Frame the discount as one-time.** "Founding-client rate" signals this isn't
  the standard rate, so it doesn't become your ceiling on the next client or a
  renewal.
- **Protect the monthly.** If you must cut somewhere, cut the one-time build, not
  the recurring plan — that's your ongoing income.
- **Hold the scope line.** Price covers what's built + the listed launch steps;
  new features are quoted separately.
