# Hero Explainer Video — Script

A short avatar/explainer for the top of the site, in the style of Peddle's
"how it works" video. Written for an AI avatar (HeyGen / Synthesia) or a
live spokesperson. Target length: **~40 seconds** (≈95–110 spoken words).

- **Tone:** friendly, local, no-pressure. Talk like a neighbor, not a call center.
- **Phone:** (937) 296-6755
- **Delivery:** keep it head-and-shoulders; smile; slow down on the numbers.

---

## Option A — 40-second spokesperson script (recommended)

> Hi! Got an old car taking up space? Here in Dayton, we turn junk cars
> into cash — fast.
>
> It's three easy steps.
>
> **One:** Tell us about your car — year, make, model, and condition. It
> takes less than two minutes, right on this page.
>
> **Two:** Get your offer. You'll see a real cash range instantly, and we
> confirm your final price by phone. No lowballing, no surprises.
>
> **Three:** We come to you. Free towing anywhere within sixty miles of
> Dayton, and you get paid on the spot — cash or check, often the same day.
>
> Running or not, wrecked or whole — we buy it. Scroll up and get your
> instant offer now, or call us at 937‑296‑6755. Let's turn that car into cash!

**Word count:** ~105 · **Est. runtime:** 38–42 sec

## Production notes

- **Avatar:** a custom avatar of the owner reads as more trustworthy than a
  stock one for a local business. ~2 min of recorded footage is enough.
- **Captions:** burn in on-screen captions — most people watch muted.
- **B-roll to intercut (optional, makes it feel like Peddle's):**
  a car in a driveway, a phone showing the offer, a tow truck, cash/check
  handed over. 2–3 short clips is plenty.
- **On-screen text cues:** "1. Tell us about your car", "2. Get your offer",
  "3. Get paid at pickup", and the phone number at the end.
- **File for the site:** export MP4 (H.264, 1080p). Landscape 16:9 for the
  hero. Keep it under ~15 MB if self-hosting, or give me a YouTube/Vimeo
  link and I'll embed instead.
